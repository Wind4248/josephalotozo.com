About Joe

I'm Joe Lotozo — a lifelong resident of Upper Arlington, Ohio, a proud Ohio State Buckeye, and a financial advisor at Edward Jones. I grew up here, went to Upper Arlington High School, and I've chosen to build my career and raise my family in the same community I've always called home.

Before joining Edward Jones in December 2012, I worked in an environment that served ultra-high-net-worth clients. That experience shaped a simple belief that drives everything I do: every person deserves that same quality of attention, guidance, and care — regardless of where they are in life. So I made a choice to bring that level of service to everyday individuals and families right here in central Ohio.

I earned my Bachelor of Arts from The Ohio State University and my Master of Science in Personal Financial Planning from the College for Financial Planning. Over the years I've added five professional designations — not because I had to, but because the people I serve deserve someone who never stops learning.

Outside the office, I'm a husband to Alaina and a dad to our daughters Lorelei and Margot. You'll find me keeping bees, tending the garden, staying active with rugby, or exploring somewhere new with my family. I believe that knowing your advisor as a person matters — and I'd love the chance to get to know you too.
